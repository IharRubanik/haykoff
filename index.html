Unbound by limitations, Martiros Manoukian has envisaged the future of art within his newest 'Constellation Collection' of mixed media paintings.

These visions were given birth, on canvas through the application of controlled combinations of organic and periodic elements, pure gold carefully crafted pigmentations and inorganic chemicals. His fascinating mixtures of color and visual effects are presented in various formats that are both enhanced and brought to life when viewed under LED lighting.
